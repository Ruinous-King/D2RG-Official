Opening Sequence

Power & Cable managment

Minotuars drop pool of power on the ground when defeated. This grants Arctian buff recieved to activate power node. Power up first node and continue powering nodes in sequence til completion. Skipping over a node or activating the wrong one will cause it to deactivate after a couple of seconds

Tips & Tricks

During this fight, Cyclops will spawn along the ridgeline while Goblins and Harpies will gather in the middle of the arena. Priortise these Cyclops as they do heavy damage.

Encounter 1: Spire Ascent

Ascend the Spire

Ascend spire by activating elevators. Follow the cables to the the nodes in sequence ensuring not to skip any nodes. This activates an evelavtor to next section. Repeat til complete.

Tips & Tricks

During this ascension, ensure there are some crowd control tools in the team as there are swarms of enemies.

Encounter 2: Akelous, the Siren’s Current

Defeat the Siren’s Current

Charge nodes to weaken Akelous. Arctrician buffs are gained from minotaurs and use to charge nodes. In order to reach a damage phase, the nodes must be charged at the center to charge the fuel rode. When all four fuel rods are activated, Akelous will go to one fuel rod and start a damage phase. Observe its eyes, destroy its red eyes before its central critical point becomes vulnerable for damage. This phase consumes a fuel rod and leaves the remain fuel rods as phases. At the end of damage phase, Akelous will perform a massive concussive blast, throwing players back and potentially off the edge. Becareful of this.

Tips & Tricks

It is possible to fall out of the arena, so ensure the team does not fight too close the edge of the arena and prepare for the concussive blast at the end of the damage phase.

Encounter 3: The Descent

Descend into the Spire

Descend a jumping puzzle into the next arena.

Tips & Tricks

A sword is useful for third person point of view for easier navigation of the puzzle.

Encounter 4: Persys, Primordial Ruin

Defeat the Primordial Ruin

Weaken Primordial Ruin and defeat it. The arena is split into a reactor room and a safe room. Use Arctrician buffs to power nodes around the arena. Nodes charge the activation of a reactor core purge, which will make Persys vulnerable. Get the Arctrician buff, then shoot the nodes on the inside of the pillars and the node above the door to access the reactor room. When reactor door opens, two nodes on the outside of the pillars will open. Locate these nodes and use the Arctrician buff to create a charge from the safe room into the reactor room. With two reactors activated, the reactor purge will occur with an on-screen prompt. Lure Persys into the reactor room and then retreat to the safe room. Seal Persys in with the reactor by shooting the node above the door. Once the purge completes, the doors will open and Persys will be vulnerable . If Persys is not in the reactor room during the purge, you will not remove its shield. Repeat the whole process until Persys is defeated.

Tips & Tricks

Persys stomps often in the tight spaces. Beware this and keep your distance as it is an easy to die.
