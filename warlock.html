Warlock Class

Stormcaller

Harmony within, hurricane without.

Overview

A crowd-control subclass focusing on controlling the hordes of darkness. Emphasis on enemy clearing and control.

Supers

Stormtrance

Throw a lightning bolt into the ground before infusing yourself with Arc energy to electrocute all enemies in your path

Chaos Reach

Manifest a torrent of Arc energy from your hand, destroying targets with a beam of pure lightning.

Grenades

  • Skip Grenade

    A grenade which splits on impact, creating multiple projectiles that seek enemies.

  • Flux Grenade

    An explosive grenade which deals additional damage when attached to enemies.

  • Arcbolt Grenade

    A grenade that chains bolts of lightning to nearby enemies.

  • Flashbang Grenade

    A grenade that bounces once and explodes, blinding enemies

  • Lightning Grenade

    A sticky, releasing lightning strikes in a vertical direction

  • Pulse Grenade

    A ball of pure arc energy that pulsates on denotation.

  • Storm Grenade

    Cast a cloud of arc energy and rain down lighting on enemies.

Abilities

  • Healing Rift

    Cast a rift of light to heal allies and yourself.

  • Empowering Rift

    Cast a rift of light to empower allies and yourself.

  • Ball Lightning

    Summon a ball of pure arc energy to strike a foe.

  • Chain Lightning

    Chain lighting between enemies once meleed.

Dawnblade

There are flames that even the Darkness cannot extinguish

Overview

A support-focused subclass for buffing team. Strong Focus on survivability and recovering health.

Supers

Daybreak

Summon a dawnblade and leap into the air to rain down fiery barrage of solar blades

Well of Radiance

Summon a dawnblade to consecrate an area to heal and empower allies.

Grenades

  • Firebolt Grenade

    Grenade detonate on contact, creating a solar flare.

  • Fusion Grenade

    An explosive grenade which deals additional damage when attached to enemies.

  • Healing Grenade

    A grenade that emits white healing light that heals allies.

  • Incendiary Grenade

    A grenade that bounces once and explodes, scorching enemies

  • Tripmine Grenade

    A sticky grenade, exploding vertical direction when enemies cross it.

  • Solar Grenade

    A dwarf sun that pulsates on denotation.

  • Thermite

    Cast a fissure that erupts, scorching the ground over time.

  • Swarm Grenade

    A Grenade that splits into eight nanites on contact, floating in a tight cluster. The nanites will track any enemies that get near the cluster.

Abilities

  • Healing Rift

    Cast a rift of light to heal allies and yourself.

  • Empowering Rift

    Cast a rift of light to empower allies and yourself.

  • Pheonix Dive

    While airborne, cast a dive to land quickly and heal allies.

  • Icarus Dash

    Envelop yourself in solar energy anddash forward.

  • Celestial Fire

    Cast three solar projecticles that explode on contact.

  • Incinerating Snap

    Snap your fingers and ignite your enemies.

Voidwalker

From the smallest atoms bloom the greatest explosions.

Overview

A damage-heavy subclass with a focus on health recovery, survivability and ability energy generation.

Supers

Novabomb

Channel and unleash an explosive, large area-of-effect attack that seeks and does damage oer time.

Novawarp

Cast a cloak of void energy to step between dimensions. Teleport between dimensions to release short-range Void eruptions to disintegrate enemies.

Grenades

  • Axion Bolt

    A bolt of Void Light that forks on impact into smaller bolts that seek out targets.

  • Magnetic Grenade:

    A grenade that attaches to targets and explodes twice.

  • Suppressor Grenade:

    An explosive grenade that suppresses, preventing targets from using abilities for a short time.

  • Scatter Grenade:

    A grenade that splits into multiple submunitions and covers a large area with multiple explosions.

  • Void Spike:

    A grenade that attaches to any surface and emits a torrent of damaging Void Light.

  • Void Wall:

    A grenade that creates a horizontal wall of burning Void Light.

  • Vortex Grenade:

    A grenade that creates a vortex that pulls targets inward and continuously damages those trapped inside.

Abilities

  • Healing Rift

    Cast a rift of light to heal allies and yourself.

  • Empowering Rift

    Cast a rift of light to empower allies and yourself.

Shadebinder

Reality is chaotic and unruly; bind it and bring order.

Overview

A support-subclass that focuses on freezing and shattering enemies to control the tide of battle. Strong focus on survivability, crowd control and enemy removal.

Supers

Winter's Wrath

Summon a Stasis staff and cast out a barrage of shards that freeze enemies and cast out a wave that shatters them

Grenades

  • Glacier Grenade

    A grenade that creates walls out of Stasis shards to block damage and freeze targets

  • Duskfield Grenade

    A grenade that shatters on impact, leaving behind a field that slows targets and freezes over time.

  • Coldsnap Grenade

    A grenade that tracks targets then freezes on impact.

Abilities

  • Healing Rift

    Cast a rift of light to heal allies and yourself.

  • Empowering Rift

    Cast a rift of light to empower allies and yourself.

  • Penumbral Blast

    Cast a shard of stasis energy from your staff forward to freeze your enemies.
