Hunter Class

Arcstrider

Flow like lightning. Strike like thunder

Overview

Melee based fighter class that focuses on heavy damage melees and abilities

Supers

Arc Staff

Form a staff of pure Arc energy and acrobatically take out your foes.

Gathering Storm

Form a javelin of pure Arc energy and impale your foes.

Grenades

  • Skip Grenade

    A grenade which splits on impact, creating multiple projectiles that seek enemies.

  • Flux Grenade

    An explosive grenade which deals additional damage when attached to enemies.

  • Arcbolt Grenade

    A grenade that chains bolts of lightning to nearby enemies.

  • Flashbang Grenade

    A grenade that bounces once and explodes, blinding enemies

  • Lightning Grenade

    A sticky grenade, releasing lightning strikes in a vertical direction

  • Pulse Grenade

    A ball of pure arc energy that pulsates on denotation.

  • Storm Grenade

    Cast a cloud of arc energy and rain down lighting on enemies.

Abilities

  • Marksman's Dodge

    Dodge to reload weapon.

  • Marksman's Dodge

    Dodge to refund melee energy.

  • Combination Blow:

    A quick strike that temporarily increases your melee damage.

  • Disorienting Blow

    Striking an enemy with this melee ability blinds them.

Gunslinger

Live for the perfect shot

Overview

High damage class that focuses on critical hits to deal massive damage to single targets.

Super

Golden Gun

Manifest a flaming gun that fires explosive light at range

Blade Barrage

Leap into the air, release a fan of daggers that explode on contact while scoraching in the surrounding proximty.

Grenades

  • Firebolt Grenade

    Grenade detonate on contact, creating a solar flare.

  • Fusion Grenade

    An explosive grenade which deals additional damage when attached to enemies.

  • Healing Grenade

    A grenade that emits white healing light that heals allies.

  • Incendiary Grenade

    A grenade that bounces once and explodes, scorching enemies

  • Tripmine Grenade

    A sticky grenade, exploding vertical direction when enemies cross it.

  • Solar Grenade

    A dwarf sun that pulsates on denotation.

  • Thermite

    Cast a fissure that erupts, scorching the ground over time.

  • Swarm Grenade

    A Grenade that splits into eight nanites on contact, floating in a tight cluster. The nanites will track any enemies that get near the cluster.

Abilities

  • Marksman's Dodge

    Dodge to reload weapon.

  • Marksman's Dodge

    Dodge to refund melee energy.

  • Radiant Dodge

    Dodge to empower allies.

  • Knife Trick

    Throw out a fan of flaming knives.

  • Lightweight Knife

    Throw a precise lightweight knife that scorches enemies.

  • Proximity Explosive Knife

    Throw a explosive knife that explodes after a short delay.

  • Weighted Throwing Knife

    Throw a massive weighted knife that critically strike foes.

Nightstalker

Draw from the Void. Light the way.

Overview

Support class used to debuff enemies and buff allies during battles. High crowd control capabilities.

Supers

Shadowshot

Summon an etheral bow to tether foes to a Void Anchor, slowing, weakening and suppressing them for you and your allies.

Spectral Blades

Summon a pair of deadly Void Blades and stalk the battlefield in a veil of shadows. Emerge from the shadows and strike.

Grenades

  • Axion Bolt

    A bolt of Void Light that forks on impact into smaller bolts that seek out targets.

  • Magnetic Grenade

    A grenade that attaches to targets and explodes twice.

  • Suppressor Grenade

    An explosive grenade that suppresses, preventing targets from using abilities for a short time.

  • Scatter Grenade

    A grenade that splits into multiple submunitions and covers a large area with multiple explosions.

  • Void Spike

    A grenade that attaches to any surface and emits a torrent of damaging Void Light.

  • Void Wall

    A grenade that creates a horizontal wall of burning Void Light.

  • Vortex Grenade:

    A grenade that creates a vortex that pulls targets inward and continuously damages those trapped inside.

Abilities

  • Marksman's Dodge

    Dodge to reload weapon.

  • Marksman's Dodge

    Dodge to refund melee energy.

  • Snare Bomb

    Throw a Smoke Bomb, which attaches to surfaces and pings enemy radar.

Revenant

From a whisper to a roar, be the calm and the storm.

Overview

Support class that controls the tide of battle with slow and freezing effects. Shattering to targets to deal damage.

Supers

Silence and Squall

Summon two Stasis kamas, Silence and Squall. Silence will lash-freeze targets on impact and Squall creates a Stasis Storm that slows and damages targets .

Grenades

  • Glacier Grenade

    A grenade that creates walls out of Stasis shards to block damage and freeze targets

  • Duskfield Grenade

    A grenade that shatters on impact, leaving behind a field that slows targets and freezes over time.

  • Coldsnap Grenade

    A grenade that tracks targets then freezes on impact.

Abilities

  • Marksman's Dodge

    Dodge to reload weapon.

  • Marksman's Dodge

    Dodge to refund melee energy.

  • Withering Blade

    Toss a Stasis Shuriken at targets to damage and slow them.

Threadrunner

The plucked string, the sharpest song. Freedom, infinitely promised.

Overview

Support and crowd control class that controls enemies movements via suspending them in place for allies.

Supers

Silkstrike

Weave a vicious rope dart and tear through your targets.

Grenades

  • Grapple

    Weave a grappling hook, and quickly pull yourself forward.

  • Threadling Grenade:

    A woven projectile that reweaves itself into Threadlings while in flight

  • Shackle Grenade

    A Strand matter that detonates on impact, suspending targets.

Abilities

  • Threaded Spike

    Throw a rope dart that bounces between targets, damaging and severing them. The dart returns after a period of time.

  • Marksman's Dodge

    Dodge to reload weapon.

  • Marksman's Dodge

    Dodge to refund melee energy.
