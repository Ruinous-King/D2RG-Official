Champions

Champion Types

Overview

Champions are Destiny 2 special class enemies that can be found in majority of end game activities. They have unique properties such as shielding or being unstoppable which requires Guardians to gear to remove them from the fight promptly.

Shield Champions

Shield champions emit an impenetrable barrier when at low health, sllowing them to heal, unless damaged with an anti-shield debuff.

Weaknesses

  • Solar Radiant
  • Void Volatile Rounds
  • Strand Unraveling Rounds

Unstoppable Champions

Unstoppable champions increase their resistance and can not be staggered when attacked by anything other than unstoppable debuff.

Weaknesses

  • Arc Blind
  • Solar Ignition
  • Stasis Shatter
  • Strand Suspend

Overload Champions

Overload champions increase their resistance and fire rate when attacked by anything other than overload debuff.

Weaknesses

  • Arc Jolt
  • Void Suppression
  • Stasis Slow
