Subclass Types

Arc Subclass

Light Based Subclass

A spark can give life . . . or take it.

Arc energy is related to electricity, electromagnetism, motion and conduction. Arc connects and disconnects all those that are in the flow of life. The balance of imbalance that is not achieveable at a cosmic level. An embodient of natural disorder and order within the universe.

Solar Subclass

Light Based Subclass

Sometimes the only answer is to burn it all away

Solar energy is related to combustion, heat and nuclear fusion, and can be wielded. Solar requires a person to both exploit and rely on the power of the universe suns, yet to master it requires them to understand a star's duality. The scorching nuclear force of anything foolish enough to enter its proximity is the same force we harness to heal. An embodiment of destruction and re-creation cycle within the universe

Void Subclass

Light Based Subclass

If you look into the void long enough, sometimes the void looks back . . .

Void is the element of cosmic distortion; the pull of gravity and the promise of entropy. That the vacuum of space and the gravitational forces that shape it show that emptiness is not nothingness. The embodiment of letting go. Void is the detachment from everything

Stasis Subclass

Darkness Based Subclass

It's stasis that kills you off in the end, not ambition . . .

The cessation of movement on a atomic level, slowing, detaining, and then shattering at all once. The embodient of utter will and control, and to wield and master it requires someone to exercise ultimate authority over both oneself and others, lest destruction run wild

Strand Subclass

Darkness Based Subclass

Weave your fate into Reality

It allows one to manipulate the Weave, a matrix of psychic energy that connects all things and minds in the universe.
