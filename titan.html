Striker

At close quarters a fist is better than any gun.

Damage heavy class focused on melee attack to remove enemies and self empower and buff.

Supers

  • Flux Grenade

    An explosive grenade which deals additional damage when attached to enemies.

  • Seismic Strike

    Strike enemies after sprinting with arc-charged explosive punch.

  • Thunderclap

    Collect arc energy into your fist and release it as explosive punch.
